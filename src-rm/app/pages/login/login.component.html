Colours Enterprise
. . . . What colour is your bottomline?

(i) We want to improve our bottomline by optimising the productivity efficiency of our human capital. We can achieve this by recording, collecting and tracking productivity data at individual staff member level in real time across the enterprise. We therefore want a tool that will help us do just that. This level of productivity accountability across the enterprise will optimise our human capital capacity and thus optimising enterprise process efficiencies. Colours is a tool which will help your enterpriss to do that. Collecting person centred productivity metrics across the enterprise means high productivity accountability in the enterpriss thus more productivity. These metrics will be like your productivity mirror and will help you continually improve your enterprise production efficiencies, translating to an improved bottomline. Colours will help your enterprise reduce its operational risk.

(ii) We want to be able to procure more busines. Knowing its real time individual staff enterprise productivity metrics, means an enterprise has the information for making the right decisions at the right time, about the changes it needs to make, to improve and get better results. The same data is used to develop an enterprises productivity profile, which enterprise then broadcasts to advertise itself to secure more business. Colours will help enterprises to procure new business through the capability to compile this enterprise productivity profile and further the Colours Marketplace platform where enterprises can publish these profiles to secure new business. All businesses want to be able to have a place where they have access to market themselves.

(iii) Enterprises want to be able to know the true performance colours of potential service providers and potential employees. Colours means that potential services providers would have had to collect their own performance data into performance profile, which they would have posted for viewing by potential Clients. The same capability means potential staff members would have to over a period of time compiled their own performance profiles. In order to secure new jobs people would have had to publish these performance profiles on Colours Marketplace. Clients and employers would then be able to view service providers and potential staff members performance profiles. Naturally persons who want to find jobs would also want to publish their performance profiles in order to secure employment.
